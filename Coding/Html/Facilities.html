OUR FACILITIES


SPA TREATMENT

The spa facilities provided in our hotel aim to provide a soothing and indulgent experience for guests after their activities. We understand that after engaging in various activities, the body and mind can feel tired and tense. Therefore, our spa is designed as the perfect place to regenerate energy and restore balance within yourself.We offer a wide range of spa treatments that are specifically designed to meet individual needs and preferences. From relaxation massages, facial treatments, body scrubs, to calming body treatments, each treatment in our spa is conducted with utmost care and using high-quality ingredients.



FITNESS CENTER

Our hotel boasts a state-of-the-art fitness center designed to cater to the wellness needs of our guests. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or looking to maintain your exercise routine while traveling, our fitness center provides a wide range of modern equipment and facilities to help you stay active and energized. From cardio machines, strength training equipment, to free weights, our fitness center offers a comprehensive selection to suit various workout preferences and fitness levels. Our knowledgeable staff is also available to assist you and provide guidance to ensure a safe and effective workout session. With a spacious and well-maintained environment,you can enjoy your fitness routine in a comfortable and motivating setting.



SWIMMING POOL

The swimming pool at our hotel is the perfect destination for guests who want to enjoy swimming without being dependent on the weather.With a warm and controlled atmosphere, our swimming pool offers a pleasant swimming experience all year round. Our pool is equipped with complete facilities, including regular and controlled water temperature, allowing guests to experience the comfort and coolness of the water without worrying about the weather outside.our indoor swimming pool also offers privacy and tranquility for guests who want to relax or swim in a quieter environment. With elegant design and beautiful lighting, our indoor pool creates a calming and luxurious ambiance.
